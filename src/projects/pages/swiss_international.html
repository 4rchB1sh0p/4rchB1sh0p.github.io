What is Beyond?

The Beyond Intensive is a one-day introduction to spiritual knowledge (Vedanta) for newcomers. Music, poetry, and open dialogue create a welcoming space for inquiry. Frank and Paula Spronk guide three sessions using the booklet "Droplets" ("Druppels"), inviting reflection on purpose, happiness, and peace of mind.

Concept plan: 11:00 entrance; 11:30 music followed by session 1; 12:30 vegetarian lunch; 13:30 music and session 2; 14:30 tea; 15:00 session 3 and music; 16:00 end. Sessions are possible in English or Dutch for about 10–20 participants. Participation is free; the booklet costs €20 (for the Mission). Dates: February 14 or 15 and February 21, 2026 (more dates later in the year).

  • Original cello compositions interwoven with silence
  • Brief spoken interludes (2–3 minutes) exploring attention and listening
  • Intimate acoustics; careful pacing for contemplation
  • No prior knowledge required—all welcome
  • A space where questions matter more than answers
