What is Beyond Tour?

Beyond is a concert-journey that blends cello, word, and stillness. Live performance meets gentle inquiry. Frank performs original compositions and classical works; Paula guides the evening with brief reflections on listening, attention, and what clarity means. It's not a lecture or sermon—it's an invitation to listen. Each evening invites a simple question: What happens when you really pay attention? No prior knowledge required. All welcome.

  • Original cello compositions interwoven with silence
  • Brief spoken interludes (2–3 minutes) exploring attention and listening
  • Intimate acoustics; careful pacing for contemplation
  • No prior knowledge required—all welcome
  • A space where questions matter more than answers

Who Are Frank & Paula?

Frank Spronk

Frank Spronk, cellist and composer, blends contemporary classical performance with thoughtful reflection. He is a direct student of a renowned 20th-century teacher known for disciplined inquiry and lucid self-knowledge. Together with Paula, he brings this lineage of clarity into the concert space, inviting audiences to listen not just to sound, but to what listening reveals.

Paula Spronk

Paula Spronk shapes the arc of each evening: pacing, context, and an atmosphere where listening becomes easy. Her background in mentoring and contemplative practice informs her role as guide. Like Frank, she studied under the same lineage of rigorous self-inquiry. Paula brings warmth, precision, and a gift for making subtle concepts accessible to all.
