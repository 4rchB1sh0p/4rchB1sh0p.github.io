What is Beyond?

Beyond is a one-day intensive that blends cello, word, and stillness. Live performance meets gentle inquiry. Frank performs original compositions and classical works; Paula guides the evening with brief reflections on listening, attention, and what clarity means.

It's not a lecture—it's an invitation to listen. Each evening asks: What happens when you really pay attention?

  • Original cello compositions interwoven with silence
  • Brief spoken interludes (2–3 minutes) exploring attention and listening
  • Intimate acoustics; careful pacing for contemplation
  • No prior knowledge required—all welcome
  • A space where questions matter more than answers

Frank & Paula
